What is AI WhatsAppBuddy?

A WhatsApp bot is a computer program or algorithm designed to interact with users on the popular messaging platform, WhatsApp. It leverages the capabilities of WhatsApp's messaging system to provide automated responses, perform tasks, and deliver information to users.

WhatsApp bots can be beneficial for businesses to automate certain tasks, provide timely information, and enhance customer engagement on the widely-used messaging platform.
