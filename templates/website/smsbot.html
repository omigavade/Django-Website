What is AI SMS Text Buddy?

An SMS bot, also known as a text message bot or SMS chatbot, is a computer program or algorithm designed to interact with users through text messages sent via Short Message Service (SMS). Unlike messaging apps like WhatsApp or Telegram, SMS bots operate through traditional cellular networks and can reach users with basic mobile phones that support SMS.

SMS bots are widely used by businesses, organizations, and service providers to automate communication, deliver information, and enhance customer experiences through SMS interactions.
