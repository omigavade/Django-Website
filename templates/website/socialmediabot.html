What is AI Instagram Buddy?

An Instagram bot is a software application or script that automates various actions on the Instagram social media platform. Instagram bots are designed to perform tasks such as liking posts, commenting on photos, following or unfollowing users, and even posting content on behalf of the user.

Instagram bots can be beneficial for businesses to automate certain tasks, provide timely information, and enhance customer engagement on the widely-used messaging platform.

What is AI Facebook Buddy?

A Facebook bot, also known as a Messenger bot or Facebook Messenger bot, is an automated software program designed to interact with users through the Facebook Messenger platform. Facebook bots leverage the messaging capabilities of Facebook to deliver automated responses, perform tasks, and provide information to users.

Facebook bots offer businesses and developers the opportunity to automate interactions, provide instant responses, and engage with users directly within the Facebook Messenger ecosystem.
