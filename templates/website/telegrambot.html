What is AI Telegram Buddy?

A Telegram bot is an automated software program that operates within the Telegram messaging platform. Telegram, a cloud-based instant messaging app, provides a platform for developers to create bots that interact with users, respond to commands, and perform various tasks.

Telegram bots have gained popularity due to their versatility, ease of use, and extensive capabilities. They have been widely adopted for personal, business, and community purposes, offering users a seamless way to interact with automated services and access information within the Telegram app.
